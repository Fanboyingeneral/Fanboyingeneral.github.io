Real-Time Rumor Verification System using AI-Powered Video Surveillance Based Threat Detection
BUET CSE Fest 2024 | 11th NSysS 2024, Khulna, Bangladesh (Dec 19-21)

Our country recently faced a mass uprising, during which the regime effectively used rumor propaganda to suppress dissent by herding violence, ambushes, and unrest among citizens. This rapid spread of misinformation highlighted the urgent need for a system capable of detecting rumors in real time.

In response, we developed a solution that combines social media text analysis with real-time surveillance footage from government CCTV systems. This integrated approach allows us to detect, categorize, and validate potential threats swiftly. Even in today's turbulent political landscape, rumors continue to fuel chaos, making our system crucial for mitigating the spread of misinformation.

Recognition: 2nd Runners Up at BUET CSE Fest 2024 | Finalist Student Poster at 11th NSysS 2024

System Overview:

Objectives: Monitor social media posts for potential threats and categorize them into 16 events, analyze real-time CCTV footage using data fusion approach, cross-reference results from social media analysis and CCTV footage, assign confidence scores to validate authenticity, and enhance public safety through real-time incident validation.

Methodology: The system continuously monitors social media posts prioritized by engagement rate and account confidence scores. Events are classified into 16 predefined categories (Abuse, Burglary, Explosion, Shooting, Fighting, Shoplifting, Road Accidents, Arson, Robbery, Stealing, Assault, Vandalism, Police Brutality, Riot/Protests, Road Blockade, and Weapon Display). Detected threats are verified against CCTV footage using cross-modal fusion based on location and time, providing a 100% confidence score for validated threats.
